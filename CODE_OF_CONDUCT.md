# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and considerate in language and tone
- Giving and accepting actionable technical feedback
- Assuming good intent while discussing disagreements

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing private information without explicit permission

## Scope

This applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, code review, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove or edit comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to project maintainers privately.
Security issues should follow `SECURITY.md`.
